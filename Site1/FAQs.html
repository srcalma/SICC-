FAQs

Ready to navigate through ins and outs of our shuttle bus services? You've come to the right place! From navigating our routes to booking your seats, and everything in between, were here to make your experience as smooth as a glide down the highway.

How do I reserve a seat on the shuttle?

To reserve a seat on the shuttle, simply visit our reservation website and fill out the online booking form. Select your origin and destination, dates, and times, and proceed to confirm your reservation.

Is the shuttle service really free?

Yes, our shuttle bus service is completely free for all passengers. There are no hidden fees or charges associated with riding the shuttle.

How often does the shuttle run?

The shuttle operates on a regular schedule, with frequent departures throughout the day. Please refer to our timetable for specific departure times.

Can I cancel my reservation after it's been confirmed?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to a certain cutoff time before the scheduled departure. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more information on deadlines and how to cancel your reservation.

Is there a limit to the number of seats I can reserve for a single trip?

Yes, you can only reserve one seat for a single trip.

Where does the shuttle pick up and drop off passengers?

The shuttle stops at designated locations throughout the campus. Please refer to our route map for more information. 

How can I provide feedback or report an issue with the shuttle service?

We welcome feedback from our passengers and encouraged you to contact us directly with any comments, suggestions or concerns. You can reach us by phone, email, or through our website.
